# Welcome to EduCore! 🌱

Hi there! We are so happy you are here. EduCore is your personal English grammar companion — like having a friendly tutor who knows exactly what you need to practice next.

## Getting Started

1. **Take the Diagnostic** (about 10 minutes)
   - Answer 25 fun questions so we can learn where you shine and where you are growing
   - There is no pass or fail — every answer teaches us something about YOU
   - Use hints if you need them — they are there to help, not to test you

2. **Start Your Daily Session** (15-20 minutes)
   - We will pick questions that are just right for your level — not too easy, not too hard
   - Each session mixes skills you are learning now with skills you have already mastered (to keep them fresh)
   - The more you practice, the smarter our choices get!

3. **Watch Your Garden Grow** 🌿
   - Every skill starts as a tiny seedling and grows through stages: growing → developing → proficient → advanced → mastered
   - Your streak shows how many days in a row you have practiced — build it up and see what happens!
   - Wrong answers are not failures — they are just steps on the path to understanding

## Tips for Success

- **Use hints freely** — they are designed to guide your thinking, not give away the answer
- **Read the explanations** — even when you get it right, the explanation might teach you something new
- **Practice a little every day** — 15 minutes daily beats 2 hours once a week
- **Celebrate progress** — notice when a skill moves from seedling to growing. That is real growth!

## Remember

> "Mistakes are just proof that you are learning something new."

You do not need to be perfect. You just need to show up, try your best, and trust that every question — right or wrong — is making you stronger.

Welcome to the journey! 🌟
