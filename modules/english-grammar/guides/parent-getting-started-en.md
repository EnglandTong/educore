# Parent Guide: Getting Started with EduCore

Welcome to EduCore! This guide will help you support your child's English grammar learning journey.

## Linking to Your Child

1. **Create your parent account** using the invite code from your child's teacher or from your child's profile screen
2. **Accept the guardian link** — your child (or their teacher) will send you an invitation
3. **Grant consent** — this lets you see their progress and communicate with their teacher

## Understanding Your Dashboard

### Progress Overview
- **Overall Score**: A number from 0-100 showing overall grammar mastery
- **Level**: Seedling → Growing → Developing → Proficient → Advanced → Mastered
- **Streak**: How many days in a row your child has practiced
- **XP Points**: Earned by answering questions — celebrate milestones!

### Skill Breakdown
Each of the 10 grammar skills has its own score. You will see:
- **Green skills** — your child is confident here
- **Yellow skills** — actively learning and improving
- **Red skills** — needs more practice and support

### Weekly Activity Chart
Shows how many minutes and questions your child completed each day. A healthy pattern is 15-20 minutes, 4-5 days per week.

## How to Help at Home

1. **Read the Subject Guides** — tap any skill name to see a parent guide with:
   - What the skill means in simple terms
   - Games and activities you can play together
   - Common mistakes (and why they are normal)
   - Signs that your child is improving

2. **Celebrate effort, not just scores** — "I love that you practiced today!" matters more than "You got 10/10"

3. **Use real-life moments** — point out grammar in everyday life: signs, menus, songs, stories

4. **Let them teach YOU** — ask your child to explain a grammar rule. Teaching deepens understanding

5. **Respect struggle** — if a skill is hard, that is where real learning happens. Do not rush past it

## Communicating with Teachers

- Tap the Messages tab to start a conversation with your child's teacher
- You can ask about specific skills, share observations from home, or request extra resources
- Teachers can see your child's detailed progress and suggest targeted practice

## Privacy & Safety

- You can only see data for children you are linked to as a guardian
- Your child cannot see YOUR conversations with teachers
- All data is encrypted and stored securely
- You can revoke consent at any time from your account settings

## Remember

> "Every name below is a whole story, not a data point."

The numbers on the screen represent a real child doing real learning. Some days will be easy, some will be hard — and that is exactly how growth works.
